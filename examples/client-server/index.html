LedgerLoops Client-Server demo (client-side part)

Mia sends 1 USD to Vincent.

That means Vincent will create a probe and send it cwise to Mia, and fwise to Marsellus. Mia forwards the cwise probe to Marsellus, but Marsellus doesn't forward either of them, because he has a zero balance on both sides, and no interest in participating in any loops (there are no liquidity incentives yet in the network).

Then, Vincent sends 1 USD to Marsellus.

That means Marsellus will create a probe and send it cwise to Vincent, and fwise to Mia.

This probe gets forwarded all the way round in both directions, and then stops just before it would have started its second round.

For now, the network participants only remember which probes they received, not which ones they sent, so it's the receiver who detects the loop: in this case, Mia detects the fwise loop, and Vincent detects the cwise loop.

Mia then starts a loop by sending a COND towards Marsellus.

Vincent could also starts a loop by sending a COND towards Mia, but by convention, only the participant who finds the fwise loop does.
